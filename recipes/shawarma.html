Shawarma

Description

Chicken shawarma is a Middle Eastern street food favorite, featuring tender, spiced chicken wrapped in warm pita bread with creamy tahini sauce. Its bold flavors come from a marinade of yogurt, garlic, and warm spices like cumin and paprika.

This recipe captures the essence of shawarma with easy-to-find ingredients, making it ideal for a quick weeknight meal or a flavorful addition to a mezze platter.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 pita breads
  • ¼ cup tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions for serving

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, olive oil, garlic, cumin, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add chicken thighs to the marinade, coat well, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  3. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place marinated chicken on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cooked through, then slice thinly.
  5. Mix tahini, lemon juice, and a splash of water to make a smooth sauce.
  6. Warm pita breads, then layer with chicken, tahini sauce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions.
  7. Roll up the pita and serve immediately.
