Pizza

Description

This classic Margherita pizza features a thin, crispy crust topped with vibrant tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil. It's a timeless dish that balances simplicity with bold flavors, perfect for a family dinner or casual gathering.

Originating from Naples, Italy, this pizza is best enjoyed fresh from the oven, with the cheese melted and bubbly. Customize with your favorite toppings, but keep it light to maintain the authentic experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out dough on a floured surface to form a 12-inch circle.
  5. Spread crushed tomatoes over the dough, sprinkle with oregano, and top with mozzarella slices.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot.
